About Our Platform

Alzheimer Care is built to support caregivers and patients in managing medications, reminders, and health updates. With a clean interface, alerts, and secure access for both, it's more than a tool — it's a helping hand.

Features

Medication Tracker

Caregiver Dashboard

Caregivers can manage schedules and access patient data in real-time.

Reminder System

Never miss a dose — automatic alerts for both patients and caregivers.

    About Our Platform

    Hope is built to support caregivers and patients in managing medications, reminders, and health updates. With a clean interface, alerts, and secure access for both, it's more than a tool — it's a helping hand.

    Features

    Medication Tracker

    Log medications with names, times, images, and notes to stay organized.

    Caregiver Dashboard

    Caregivers can manage schedules and access patient data in real-time.

    Reminder System

    Never miss a dose — automatic alerts for both patients and caregivers.
